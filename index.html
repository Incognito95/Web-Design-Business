Frequently Asked Questions

  • What's the process?

    First we jump on a 30 minute call to gather information, figure out what kind of website you want and what kind of layout, how many pages you need, as well as estimating how long it's going to take to complete everything. Once everything is agreed upon then a contract will be sent to you highlighting what we have discussed in terms of what kind of website it is, how long it will take, fee etc etc...

  • How much does it cost?

    You can see the cost in the packages section

  • How is the payment structure?

    I would require 50% of the whole fee upfront before beginning the process of the website after having the first call, then once the website is completed, website is launched and all the project files are handed over to you I would need the remainder of the fee

  • What type of payments do you accept?

    I accept MobilePay, PayPal or Bank Transfer

  • Will I receive all the project files?

    Yes you will be given all the project files in a zip folder if your website is created in Wordpress, if it's created in Squarespace you will get transferred the ownership of the site over to you. Once the whole website is complete everything is yours and I will be showing and guinding you through how your able to edit your own content
